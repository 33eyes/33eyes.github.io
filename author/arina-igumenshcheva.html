About

Hi! I am a data scientist who is passionate about using statistics and machine learning techniques to discover meaningful insights in messy data. I have spent several years working with data, first as a data analyst for a fraud detection software solution, and later as an information systems programmer at a medical research lab. Most recently, I have completed the Metis Data Science bootcamp, where I've studied the latest statistics and machine learning techniques.
